About This Page

We all know how K-pop, K-drama, K-beauty and many more are spreading wild like fire and people are getting more curious to know about South Korea, their culture, tradition and basically anything that is related to their favourite K-pop Idol. Well, being one of the admirers of their culture I decided to make this page in which basically you’ll be able to find anything you are looking for.
At the moment I am still working on this page but I assure you that all the contents are going to be timely updated and will be pertinent.

What is Hallyu?

Hallyu or the Korean wave is the increase in global popularity of South Korean culture all around the world. Now we all have witnessed some of the record-breaking achievements of different K-pop groups in the recent times whether it be Guinness World Records or billboard music charts, Korean pop culture is dominating. They have a huge impact on their fans and these are mainly carried through the internet and social media. There are tons of stories about how fans have helped, supported and influenced people all around the globe. Some say that their Idol’s music has helped them to stay focused, to some listening to K-pop brings them pleasure but most importantly the message that these songs contain reach outs to people’s heart. It spreads a little more positivity and love in this world full of self-centred humans. Many fandoms have helped raised money which resulted in great relief for the needy in the time of crisis. In 2018, the Mukbang trend flooded the YouTube feed. Literally every youtuber was uploading a Mukbang video. Through these videos many came to know about Mukbang and where it initially started, well you guessed it right, South Korea.
